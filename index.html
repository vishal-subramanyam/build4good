Our Southeast Asian Delights

Pad Thai

A classic Thai stir-fried noodle dish with tamarind sauce, shrimp, tofu, bean sprouts, and crushed peanuts.

Green Curry

A fragrant Thai curry made with coconut milk, green chili, basil, and your choice of protein (chicken, beef, or tofu).

Nasi Goreng

Indonesian fried rice seasoned with sweet soy sauce, garlic, shallots, and topped with a fried egg.

Banh Mi

A Vietnamese sandwich filled with grilled meat (usually pork), pickled vegetables, fresh herbs, and a crusty baguette.

Laksa

A spicy Malaysian soup with coconut milk, rice noodles, shrimp, chicken, and bean sprouts.
